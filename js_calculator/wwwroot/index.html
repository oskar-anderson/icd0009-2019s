Rules

The game starts with phase 1 also know as the placement phase. In the placement phase players take turn placing their 12 stones onto the empty cells of the board. Making three-in-a-row vertically or horizontally during the placement phase is not allowed. After all 24 stones have been placed phase 2 the swap phase begins.

In the swap phase players swap 2 orthogonally adjacent stones. The goal is to get a three-in-a-row, four or more in a row is not allowed. If a player forms three-in-a-row then they enter phase 3 the removal phase.

In the removal phase players get to remove 1 of the opponent's stones. If a player had formed two or more three-in-a-rows in one move then only one enemy piece will be removed. Removing a opponent's piece that is part of a three-in-a-row is not allowed.

The player that has 2 pieces left loses.
